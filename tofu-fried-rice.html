Tofu Fried Rice

Steaming plate of Tofu Fried Rice, garnished with toasted sesame seeds and peanuts

This somple recipe is wonderfully easy to cook, and ridiculously simple. Although this isn't a recipe I developed with my sister during the pandemic, it is a recipe I used merely hours ago to cook dinner for myself, and hence the only recipe fresh in my mind right now.

Ingredients

  • Sesame seeds
  • peanuts
  • Tofu
  • Rice
  • veggies (cauliflower, carrots, peas, etc.)
  • Soy sauce
  • Red chilli sauce
  • Green chilli sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Honey
  • Corn starch
  • Garlic powder
  • 1 onion
  • Salt
  • MSG

Steps

  1. In a dry pan, toast sesame seeds and peanuts together for 5 minutes.
